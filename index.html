About Me

Creating learning that you experience, rather than simply complete.

I’m Graham Betts, a Digital Learning Experience Manager who designs learning that respects the learner’s time — and drives performance.

I create engaging learning experiences to create change, not just to drive completion. My focus is on practical, scenario-led design that helps colleagues build confidence, apply skills on the job, and achieve measurable outcomes.


What I do best:


  • Learning Experience Design (LXD): Interactive, scenario-based learning that builds real capability
  • Complexity made clear: Technical and regulated topics translated into engaging, digestible learning journeys
  • SAP SuccessFactors LMS transformation: Improving the end-to-end learner experience (look & feel, navigation, structure, discoverability and adoption)
  • Data-led design: Using analytics and stakeholder insight to shape content, journeys and evaluation
  • Delivery & leadership: Experienced working solo and leading remote, matrix and onsite teams

My philosophy is simple: Learning is the process of applying knowledge, not just consuming it.strong>
