About Me

Creating learning that you experience, rather than simply complete.

I'm Graham Betts, an Digital Learning Professional passionate about creating learning experiences that respect the learner's time and intelligence.

In my career ive operated solo, and led teams, both onsite, remotely, and offshore. Whether on my own or driving a group of individuals, it has always been with the goal of using data to link business needs, learner skills and knowledge, to tangible actions and activities that support personl growth. With a background in both technical training and creative design, I specialize in translating dry, complex subject matter into interactive, digestible content. My philosophy is simple: Effective learning is active, not passive.
