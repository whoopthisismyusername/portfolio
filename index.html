About Me

The person behind the work.

I love L&D. I also love problem solving with creative solutions. The current shift in the learning space towards performance enablement is an incredible space to be in. I believe that technology, data and creativity work hand in hand to solve the challenges modern workforces are having. By following clear processes and working closely with learners and business experts, it is possible to unlock the true potential of a workforce in the pace that is required to stay ahead.

In my personal life, these same themes carry through. A lover of speciality coffee, I thrive in finding that perfect grind size and recipe through trial and error and educated problem solving, leading to moments of tasty magic. I'm also a massive geek who loves lego and doing this stuff for fun!

  • SuccessFactors LMS: Improving end-to-end learner experience
  • Data-led design: Analytics & stakeholder insight shaping content
  • Leadership: Solo and leading remote/matrix/onsite teams
