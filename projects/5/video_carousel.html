Example 1 of 3
1

Health & Safety Smartphone Video

Following the reduction of Covid restrictions the Health & Safety team required support in demonstrating safe practices for returning to the workplace. This required me mapping out and storyboarding a series of videos in a way to require minimal human contact, little to no support and with extremely basic tech access. This was then all edited at pace to be concise in it's delivery. The result, a series of videos and a successful course that displayed all necessary safe practices.

2

Animated Communications

This video was a short and snappy video used to promote and educate significant new changes to a compliance training programme. The video displays visual iconography to clearly illustrate the process and dates. Following the release of this video and the launch of the process, there was high engagement with the launch of the new process and lower than forecast support queries.

3

Customer Service Interactive Video

This video formed an interactive stage of sales through service training. It involved the hiring of actors and the use of an external filming agency. All storyboards and scripts were developed by myself and the final edit work completed by myself using After Effects.
