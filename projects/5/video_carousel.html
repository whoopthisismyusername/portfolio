Design Process: Infographics & QRGs

Step 1 of 3
1

Analyzing the Problem

We identified that call center agents were overwhelmed by 15+ pages of dense pricing tables. Heatmap analysis revealed they only accessed 20% of the data regularly, leading to long hold times.

2

Drafting the Solution

I drafted a new color-coded structure using Adobe InDesign. We condensed the 15 pages into a single, interactive PDF. Key information was prioritized based on frequency of use.

3

Implementation & Review

The final QRG was deployed to the Intranet. User testing confirmed that information retrieval speed improved by 40%. The design is now the template for all future job aids.
